Caprese Salad

Home

Looking for a quick and easy meal during the week? Look no further!

Caprese Salad is a staple for me, mostly due to how delicious and quick it is!

Ingredients

  • 100g Balsamic Vinegar
  • 25g Honey
  • 3 Large Tomatoes, cut into half slices
  • 2 packages of fresh mozzarella, cut in quarter slices
  • Salt
  • 10g of basil leaves
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients and then mix the balsamic and honey together in a small saucepan.
  2. Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low, simmer until mixture has reduced significantly (about 10 mins). Then set aside to cool.
  3. Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella neatly on a serving platter.
  4. Sprinkle with black pepper, tuck basil leaves around the platter, drizzle with olive oli and the balsamic reduction.
