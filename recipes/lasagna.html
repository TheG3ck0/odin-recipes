Lasagna recipe

Home

Nothing beats a good homemade lasagna -- this one is no exception!

This lasagna may take a while but it's all worth it in the end, a warm dish full of home cooked goodness!

Ingredients

  • 500g of mince meat
  • 1/2 a diced onion
  • 2 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • 250ml of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Chopped Parsley (2 tablespoons)
  • 4 teaspoons of italian seasoning
  • A pinch of black pepper and salt
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 250g of ricotta cheese
  • 100g of mozzarella
  • 50g of parmesan (or pecorino) cheese

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook together the mince meat, onion and garlic in spacious pan over medium heat until very browned.
  3. Stir in the, crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water.
  4. Season with, sugar, parsley, basil, a pinch of salt, italian seasoning and black pepper. Simmer, covered for about 1hr 30 mins, whilst stirring occasionally
  5. If lasagna noodles need to boiled, do that for this step, if not move on to step 6. Boil some water, add a pinch of salt and cook your noodles for about 8-10 minutes, then drain your noodles once finished
  6. Combine ricotta cheese with 2 tablespoons of parsley and a pinch of salt
  7. Preheat oven to 190 degrees c
  8. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat along the bottom of a 9x13 inch dish. Arrange the lasagna noodles lengthwise on the top of the meat, then add ricotta cheese, parmesan and mozzarella. Repeat layers until you have run out of ingredients.
  9. Bake in oven for about 50 minutes, checking halfway through
  10. Let rest for 20 minutes, then serve!
