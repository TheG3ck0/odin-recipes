Lasagna recipe

Home

Nothing beats a good homemade lasagna -- this one is no exception!

This lasagna may take a while but it's all worth it in the end, aa warm dish full of home cooked goodness!

Ingredients

  • 500g of mince meat
  • 1/2 a diced onion
  • 2 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • 250ml of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Chopped Parsley (2 tablespoons)
  • 4 teaspoons of italian seasoning
  • A pinch of black pepper and salt
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 250g of ricotta cheese
  • 100g of mozzarella
  • 50g of parmesan (or pecorino) cheese
